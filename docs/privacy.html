Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Verve ("we," "us") builds the Verve app, which helps people discover and plan activities nearby. This policy explains what information Verve collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

CategoryWhat, and why
Account infoEmail address and password if you sign up directly, or your name/email via Sign in with Apple (Apple may provide a private relay email instead of your real one — that's Apple's choice, not ours).
LocationYour device's precise location, used to find activities and events near you. You can also manually search a different city instead of sharing your location.
PhotosPhotos you choose to attach to a review or a community event you post.
Content you createReviews and ratings, saved places and trip boards, group hangout votes and chat messages, and community event listings you submit.
Reports & blocksIf you report content or block another user, we store who was reported/blocked and why, so we can act on it.

We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not run ads in Verve.

How we use this information

  • To show you relevant nearby activities and events
  • To operate features you use directly — reviews, saved boards, group hangouts and voting, chat, community event listings
  • To let you and others report or block abusive content or users
  • To respond to support requests

Who we share it with

We don't sell your data. We share the minimum necessary with the services that make Verve work:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Google Places API — we send your coordinates to Google to look up nearby places; we don't send your name, email, or account details.
  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — used to show currently-playing movies; no personal information is sent.
  • Apple — if you use Sign in with Apple, Apple handles authentication directly.

Other users of Verve can see content you choose to share publicly within the app — reviews, community event listings, and messages within a hangout you've joined.

Your choices

  • Edit or delete a review any time from the activity page.
  • Block another user from a report dialog anywhere in the app, or manage existing blocks from Profile → Privacy & Safety.
  • Sign out from Profile → Account.
  • Delete your account from Profile → Account → Delete account. This removes your reviews, saved places, boards, and hangout activity, and queues your account for permanent deletion.

Children's privacy

Verve is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
