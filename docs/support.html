Common questions

  • How do I delete my account? Open Vervi → Profile → Account → Delete account.
  • How do I block someone or report content? Long-press a chat message, or tap "Report" on a review or community event.
  • How do I unblock someone? Profile → Privacy & Safety → Blocked users.
